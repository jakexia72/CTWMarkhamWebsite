Executive Applications Out Now!

2/6/17

IMVC/CTW Youth Ambassador Position

 

WANTED! People between the ages of 14-18 who;

  • love volunteering!
  • believe youth have a positive impact on our communities
  • are highly engaged with volunteering and their community
  • want to create greater, more enjoyable and exciting opportunities for other young people to become engaged with their community and volunteering!

 

IMVC and CTW Youth Ambassadors will bring youth volunteering and community engagement to life.  This 6 month position is unique, offers a depth of experience and provides you with a platform to learn invaluable skills, develop volunteering/civic engagement events and engage with your community.

 

As a Youth Ambassador your role will be to:

  • Make volunteering come alive in your community!
  • Promote the ChangeTheWorld- Ontario Youth Volunteer Challenge in your schools and communities
  • Inspire your peers and other youth to engage in volunteerism
  • Help create events and volunteer opportunities for youth in your community

As a Youth Ambassador you will receive:

  • An incredible volunteer experience and the opportunity to engage in real change and civic action in your community
  • Support from IMVC  in creating events and developing volunteering opportunities for young people in your community
  • A minimum of 40 volunteer hours
  • A Certificate of Achievement from IMVC and CTW including a PREB Certificate outlining your skills/experience
  • A reference for other volunteer opportunities, college/university or career

As a Youth Ambassador you must be able to:

  • Virtually or physically meet with staff and other Youth Ambassadors twice per month or as required
  • Travel within your community
  • Create/support at least 1 volunteer project with other youth in your school or community during the ChangeTheWorld campaign (April 10 – May 23)
  • Assist with the creation and delivery of events related to the ChangeTheWorld campaign
  • Engage in social and other media to promote IMVC and the ChangeTheWorld campaign

To receive more information and the application form please email Mirja Raita at markhamyouthchallenge@gmail.com
