FAQ

Look here for some frequently asked questions to save yourself some time.

▸ How do I sign up to volunteer?

1. Choose the event you want to participate in and click on the button.
2. Write an email that includes your full name and the name of the event in the subject line. To sign up for more than one event, simply add another event name in the subject line.
3. Wait for the confirmation email and your in!

▸ What if I can't make the event I signed up for?

Do not simply not show up. We understand that sometimes your schedule is out of your control, but when you confirm, we give you a specially reserved, limited, spot. But if you really can't make it, please email us at least 3 days before the event so that we can find another volunteer to take your place.

▸ How do I get my volunteer hours?

As a student in Ontario, you are required a minimum of 40 hours of community service. Thankfully, all of our events can add to you community service hours. When you volunteer for one of our events, we will give you the hours by the end. Just don't forget your volunteer form!

▸ How do I get to my event?

Unless otherwise specified, you are responsible getting to the site yourself.

▸ When should I arrive at my event?

Unless otherwise specified, please always be onsite atleast 15 minutes before the event starts, this way we can check in with you and prepare you.

▸ What is the photo policy?

You can take all the photos you want and please spread the word(unless you are asked not to). Keep in mind that, we too will be photographing our events and by volunteering at our events you agree that we can take photos of you.
