Trudeau Robotics

Formally known as Trubotics, our highschool robotics team was one of my favourite clubs. As and exec, I was head of publicity and design. I used catchy graphics and illustrations to instill a sense of innovation, attracting new memebers and creating a cohesive brand identity. Of course, I did actually work on the robots too - both as builder and a programmer.

World Hepatitis Day 2017

In 2017, I interned at the Asian Liver Centre at Stanford for a summer. In Asia, Hep B and liver cancer is rampant due to lack of vaccination and stigma. World Hepatitis Day is a public health campaign created to educate and fight stigma, especially in the eastern parts of the world. To aid this movement, I created many publications and PSAs in collaboration with the CDC to show to the chinese public in Beijing.
